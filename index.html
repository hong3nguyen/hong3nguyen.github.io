Hi! I'm Tri Hong Nguyen, a postdoctoral researcher at The Aalto Systems and Services Engineering Analytics (AaltoSEA) Group at the Aalto University .

When studying at the University of Information Technology, Vietnam National University - Ho Chi Minh City (UIT, VNU-HCMC), I found my interest topics related to information security with my friends. At that time, we formed a CTF team and played together. Then, I stepped forward to an advanced class to change my idea to follow the academic path until now.
